Game Rules

In this game, the AI has picked a secret character and goal. Your job is to figure out who the character is based on clues provided by the AI. You will interact with the AI and receive hints through conversation. If you're stuck or want to give up, simply let the AI know, and it will end the game!

Work out who you are based on the clues, and remember, if you want to give up, just say so!

Good luck!
